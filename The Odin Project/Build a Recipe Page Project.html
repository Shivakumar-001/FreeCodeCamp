Creamy Chocolate Fudge

Description

Also called cocoa liquor, this is the base of all chocolate and is made from cocoa bean nibs. It's a thick, dark brown paste that turns into a liquid when heated.

Chocolate fluidity: The percentage of cocoa butter in chocolate determines its fluidity. Chocolate with more cocoa butter is more fluid and will form a thinner shell when melted
